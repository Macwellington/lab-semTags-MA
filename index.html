Silicon Chip Implant Marvel

Researchers at Indiana University are developing silicon nanochips that can reprogram skin cells to become blood vessel cells.

This technology could reduce the need for traditional chemical treatments and improve recovery from injuries.

Legal Implications, IU School Of Medicine
